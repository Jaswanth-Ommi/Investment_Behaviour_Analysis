* Generated summary KPIs
* Analyzed savings objectives

### Visualizations Created

* Most common savings objectives

## ✅ Task 2 – Gender-Based Investment Analysis

### Objective

Analyze investment preferences based on gender.

### Analysis Performed

* Equity Market comparison
* Mutual Funds comparison
* Government Bonds comparison
* Gender-wise average investment analysis

### Key Findings

* Male investors showed higher interest in Equity Markets
* Female investors preferred Mutual Funds more
* Government Bonds were preferred more by male investors
